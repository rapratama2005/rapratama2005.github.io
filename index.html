Start of Winter Break

Dec 20, 2021
Image

So, it's finally here. Winter break. I'll be honest it did not feel like an entire semester but that could be a product just how fast time flies.

While we're here, it might be a good idea to reflect about this semester, all of it's ups and downs.

On the downside: OH GOD THAT'S A LOT OF WORK FROM TWO AP CLASSES (APCSA is easy).
On the upside: Video Game playtime has gone down, Guitar time went up, and I am now Underclassmen Representative of the CS Club and Vice President of the Robotics Team.

Overall, probably a net positive. Now, what are the plans for next semester?

I want to get better on one-on-one conversations. I don't think I'm particulary good at that, and I need to be more social. I'll probably go to more CS and Robotics competitions. There's a good chance that I'm running for an NHS officer position, because why not?
